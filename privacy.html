Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 8, 2026

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Long Trail 433 LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our internal QuickBooks Online integration application (the "Application"). This Application is used solely for internal business purposes and is not offered to the general public.

2. Information We Access

Through our integration with QuickBooks Online, the Application accesses the following types of financial data from our own QuickBooks Online account:

  • Chart of accounts and account balances
  • Customer and vendor records
  • Invoices, bills, and payment records
  • Journal entries and transaction history
  • Employee records

3. How We Use Information

All data accessed through the Application is used exclusively for internal accounting and rental property management purposes, including:

  • Tracking rental income and expenses
  • Managing vendor and tenant records
  • Generating financial reports
  • Reconciling accounts

4. Data Storage and Security

The Application operates on local, privately controlled infrastructure. QuickBooks Online API credentials (OAuth 2.0 tokens) are stored securely in environment variables on local machines. No financial data is transmitted to or stored on third-party servers beyond QuickBooks Online itself.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose any data accessed through the Application to any third parties. Data remains solely within our internal systems and QuickBooks Online.

6. Data Retention

The Application does not independently store or cache QuickBooks Online data. All data is accessed in real time through the QuickBooks Online API and resides within our QuickBooks Online account.

7. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates solely with QuickBooks Online, provided by Intuit Inc. Use of QuickBooks Online is subject to Intuit's Privacy Policy.

8. Your Rights

As this Application is for internal use only and does not collect data from external users, there is no external user data to manage. If you have questions about this policy, please contact us using the information below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
